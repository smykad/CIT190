Wanted Poster


This page is for you to upload your own photo and name to create your very own wanted poster! It's incredibly simple to use, even your grandma can do it! First you just have to click the upload image button to add your own image [A png works best for this!] and then you just enter your name in the text box and click add name and voila, you're very own wanted poster!
